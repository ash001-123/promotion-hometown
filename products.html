Matabungkay Beach Resort Beach Paradise

Matabungkay Beach Hotel has been a top destination for local and foreign tourists wanting to get away from it all for over 30 years. With its comfortable accommodations, wide range of outdoor activities, pampering services, and music and sports lounge, Matabungkay provides the total resort experience. Matabungkay, a town situated along the idyllic coastal town of Lian in Batangas, is a popular area for water sports such as swimming, diving, boating and snorkeling. It has a 2 kilometre stretch of good beach and sand.

San Diego Beach Resort Clear Water

If you are looking for a beach where you can relax, and enjoy activities, here you are at San Diego Beach Resort, which has clean and clear water, and offers a variety of activities, You can climb a mountain to see more the real beauty of San Diego.

BoardWalk Hidden Paradise

Are you a nature lover? Well here's our one of a great tourist attraction that we are very proud of. The very known Boardwalk. It is located at Kay Reyna Lumaniag Lian Batangas. It has one kilometer-walk bridge made of bamboo sorrounded by mangrove trees. A perfect place for photo shoot like pre-nup or pre-debut photoshoot. There is an amazing big rock formation that can captivate your full attention.
